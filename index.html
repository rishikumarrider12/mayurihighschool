About Mayuri Vidyalayam

25+ Years of Excellence in Education

A Sanctuary for Learning and Growth

Mayuri Vidyalayam is a premier educational institution dedicated to fostering academic brilliance, character formation, and holistic student growth. Located in a tranquil environment in Kowthala, Adilabad, we combine traditional educational values with modern pedagogical techniques.

Our Vision

To cultivate intellectual curiosity, leadership quality, and ethical character, shaping pupils into global leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous, supportive academic environment staffed by exceptional teachers who inspire children to reach their full potential.
